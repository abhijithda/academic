Profile:              

               Deutsche Software (India) is a  wholly owned s ubsidiary of Deutsche Bank ( Asia-Pacific ) Training Centre Pvt.Ltd.It  was established in 1992. The company  showed a revenue of  Rs.43.32 crore in  the year 1999 -2000 showing a growth  of about  11%.  The company  has  its headquaters  at  Bangalore.  Deutsche  Software  presently  has  about  400 employees. Mr.S.Nagarajan is the present CEO.

            It is a software  company  that  is  much more specific to providing financial management services to banking and other financial  institutions. Deutsche Software provides IT  support  to  Deutsche Bank  AG WorldBank , and  also other subsidaries of its.

The company offers a remuneration of Rs.2.4 to 2.6 lacs per annum.
